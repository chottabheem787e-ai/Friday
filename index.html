Configuration
How to install as Android app:
1. Open this HTML file in Chrome on your Android
2. Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮) in Chrome
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap Add — FRIDAY icon appears on your home screen!

For voice to work: Chrome will ask for microphone permission — tap Allow.
